Dream Glasses

Illustration print series

Initial illustrations were done with pen and ink in my sketchbook. Bringing those drawings into illustrator and vectorized them, I then played around with copies of each and added more shapes and lines to pull the final illustration together. I put these designs into redbubble.com and society6.com for the item application.

Trunk album cover & booklet design

created for Ottawa band album cover and booklet

objective was to create a piece of digital art for each song on the album, specific to the vibe and aesthetic of the song. I then had to incorporate each piece of art on to the front cover in a way it would all flow together. I used my own photography and edited and manipulated it using a combination of Lightroom, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

created for wine and liquor

created this alcohol brand by making a custom label on a wine bottle for a friend as a gift. after sketches and mockups, I put this version in Illustrator and tweaked it until satisfied. planning to use this label in the future for personal wine/liquor making.

Haunt : Photography series

concept and photographs : jess paget // help and model : amelinda everett

series created to portray the haunting feelings of guilt, fear, anxiety, or anything that may haunt a human in their daily life. I chose to take these photos with my Polaroid camera instead of my SLR because it leaves the photos looking old, grainy, and even a little bit dingy which fit the series perfectly. I then scanned and edited using Lightroom.

nature and long exposure photographs

over the spring, summer, and fall I have acquired a series of photographs of the wonders of the sky. from cottage trips, the super moon/eclipse, and just breath taking sunsets. most of them using long exposure setting on my Nikon D90.

2001 : a space odyssey movie poster

databending application

took a jpeg2000 and brought it into text edit and changed around the coding of the photograph to end up with the background of this poster. I then chose a movie poster to re-create to use the photo in a way that would accurately portray it. Added the shapes and text in Illustrator.
